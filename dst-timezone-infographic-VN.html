üí° Key Insight: Summer Time = Next Zone East

A region's summer time (DST) uses the same UTC offset as the standard time of the zone immediately to its east:

  • AZOST (UTC+0) = GMT/WET standard time
  • BST/IST/WEST (UTC+1) = CET standard time
  • CEST (UTC+2) = EET standard time
  • EEST (UTC+3) = MSK standard time
